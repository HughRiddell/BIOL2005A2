Step 01 · Baseline condition

The standard task.

Run this for every participant before configuring the experimental condition. The parameters below are fixed.

Instructions

On every trial you will see a small fixation cross in the middle of the screen, then a cloud of moving dots. Most dots move randomly, but some move coherently to the left or right. Your task is to decide which.

After the dots disappear, the screen will go blank. That is when you respond. Press the ← key for left, the → key for right. There is no feedback — just respond as accurately as you can and move on.

Try not to think too long. If you are unsure, give your best guess. The trials are short, and there are 120 of them; the session takes around six to seven minutes.

Tip Keep your eyes fixed on the cross in the centre of the screen, even during the dot motion. Do not try to track individual dots.
